About Our NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on September 23, 2020, by Govind Shukla and based in Chhattisgarh, India. The foundation is certified with 80G, 12A, CSR-1, NITI Aayog registration, and ISO 9001:2015, ensuring transparency, accountability, and quality service.

Social Impact and Purpose of Projects

The projects are designed to improve the lives of underprivileged communities and create a positive social impact across India. Through food distribution, education support, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development programs, the foundation aims to promote equality, compassion, sustainability, and self-reliance. These initiatives help provide basic necessities, encourage learning opportunities, support financial independence, protect animals and nature, and enhance employment opportunities for individuals in need. The foundation believes that collective efforts and community participation can build a more inclusive, empowered, and socially responsible society.
